FAQ - Section

How do I choose the right lipstick shade for my skin tone?
Select warm tones like corals and reds for warm skin tones, and cool tones like pinks and berries for cool skin tones.
What's the best way to make lipstick last longer?
Apply a lip primer, line your lips, fill them in with a lip pencil, and then apply your lipstick. Blot, reapply, and set with translucent powder for long-lasting color.
Can I mix lipstick colors to create a custom shade?
Absolutely! Experimenting with mixing lipstick colors can lead to unique and personalized shades that complement your style.
How can I prevent lipstick from feathering or bleeding?
Use a lip liner to outline your lips and prevent feathering. Opt for matte formulas, and consider applying a small amount of concealer around your lips.
What's the best lipstick finish for a natural look?
For a natural look, go for a satin or sheer finish. These provide a hint of color without being too bold.
How can I remove long-wearing or matte lipsticks easily?
Use an oil-based makeup remover or coconut oil to gently break down and remove stubborn long-wearing or matte lipsticks.
